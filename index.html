Meet the Tablers

A diverse and dynamic group bringing fellowship, service, leadership, and personality to London West End 623.

Ryan Zammit The Logistics Chief

A man of refined, mechanical tastes—if it has an engine, a track, or a DRS wing, Ryan is interested. When he isn’t navigating his role as an IT Delivery Manager, he’s likely plotting his next travel escape or perfecting the art of the professional TV binge.

Matteo The Train Enthusiast

A veteran Tabler with over five years of fellowship and roots at Rugby 113. He loves social chaos and community impact and has helped deliver legendary fireworks events raising over £100k for local causes.

Amit Shah The Tech Nomad

Software Engineer and digital explorer. From craft beer and hidden-gem restaurants to films, games, and coding challenges, Amit blends curiosity and discipline with a passion for global travel.

Steve The Foundation

One of the Phoenix founders who helped relaunch 623 in 2019. A Chartered Surveyor and sports enthusiast, Steve is the bedrock of the Table and happiest outdoors, whether on the golf course or hiking trails.

John Bergqvist The Forest Fellow

Immediate Past Chairman (2025-26), originally from Sweden. A scientist at Imperial specializing in worms, John brings curiosity, fellowship, and strategic thinking to every conversation and event.

Kyle "Mitchi" West The Workshop Wizard

Unapologetically nerdy and proud, Kyle brings DnD, anime, and comics energy to the Table. By day he supervises workshop operations in suitcase repair, and by night he strengthens the brotherhood spirit.

Vedang Tyagi The Global Heir

A doctor by profession and a wholehearted “yes man” socially, Vedang grew up around Table in India. He balances clinical life with hosting, sport, travel, and relentless curiosity for learning.

David Crow The West End Maverick

Always in motion, David moves between countries, city events, and creative spaces. One night he is on a red carpet, the next behind the decks as a DJ, always bringing stories and energy.

Amrinder Chana Area 6 Legend in London

With over 14 years in Table and leadership across club and area level, Amrinder’s journey includes recognition as RTBI Area Chairman of the Year (2017-18). He now leads Area 26 for 2026-27.

Jon Clark The International Envoy

A founding member of London West End 623 and National President for RTBI (2025-26), Jon has led at every level. His leadership, fellowship, and international perspective make him a cornerstone of modern Table life.

Jay Shah The Global Adrenaline Hunter

A powerhouse in Table leadership across Eastern Africa and London, Jay combines business, tech, and social impact. From ultramarathons to mountain climbs, he is always chasing the next challenge.
