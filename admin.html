Gallery

Upload photos from your events here. They'll appear in the photo gallery section of your website.

ℹ️ How to use this panel

What this does: Manages photo albums that appear on your website's Gallery page.

Step by step:

  1. Click "+ New Album" to create an album for an event
  2. Give it a name (e.g. "Summer Social 2026")
  3. Click "Manage photos" to upload images
  4. Drag photos or click Browse to select files
  5. Add captions to describe each photo

💡 Tips:

  • Use landscape photos (wider than tall) for best results
  • Keep file sizes under 5MB (large files are slow to load)
  • Add descriptive captions so visitors know what they're looking at

Programme

Add upcoming events to your calendar. These show on the homepage so members know what's coming up.

ℹ️ How to use this panel

What this does: Manages your club's event calendar shown on the homepage.

Step by step:

  1. Click "+ Add event" to create a new event
  2. Fill in the date, time, location, and event details
  3. Add a contact person so members know who to ask
  4. Click "Save Changes" to publish to your website

💡 Tips:

  • Events are shown in date order automatically
  • Past events won't show on the homepage (they auto-hide)
  • Include full location details (venue name + address)

Tablers

Manage member profiles that appear on the "Meet the Team" page of your website.

ℹ️ How to use this panel

What this does: Creates and updates member profiles on your website.

Step by step:

  1. Click "+ Add tabler" to add a new member
  2. Enter their name and role (e.g. "Chairman", "Secretary")
  3. Upload a photo or paste a photo URL
  4. Write a short bio (2-3 sentences)
  5. Click "Save Changes" to publish

💡 Tips:

  • Professional headshots work best (square photos)
  • Keep bios friendly and approachable
  • Include what the person does for the club

Guestbook

Review and approve messages from visiting Tablers before they appear on your website.

ℹ️ How to use this panel

What this does: Lets you moderate guestbook entries before they go live.

Step by step:

  1. New entries appear in the "Pending" column
  2. Read the message to check it's appropriate
  3. Click "Approve" to publish it on your website
  4. Click "Delete" to remove spam or inappropriate messages

💡 Tips:

  • You'll see a badge showing how many entries need review
  • Approved entries appear on your public guestbook page immediately
  • You can approve multiple entries at once if they're all good
